## Notes

- Cards should feel like durable product surfaces, not decorative tiles.
- Use cards for repeated entities and framed tools only.

## Implementation Ideas

- Use the shared `Card`, `Panel`, badge, and skeleton primitives.
- Keep radii, borders, shadows, and hover states tokenized.

## Performance Considerations

- Keep hover effects transform-only where possible.
- Avoid nested card stacks.

## Adaptation Ideas For MatchIQ

- Pair graphite surfaces with thin borders, blue-violet highlights, and concise information hierarchy.
