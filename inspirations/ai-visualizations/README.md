## Notes

- AI visuals should imply analysis, ranking, reasoning, and career intelligence.
- Avoid generic particle clouds and decorative neural-network filler.

## Implementation Ideas

- Use lightweight gradients, graph-like lines, score rings, and explanation trails.
- Keep visualizations tied to real product data.

## Performance Considerations

- Prefer CSS/SVG for simple intelligence visuals.
- Defer canvas/WebGL until a specific high-value use case exists.

## Adaptation Ideas For MatchIQ

- Represent fit, momentum, gaps, and opportunity quality as structured intelligence layers.
